About DeepGuard AI

Our Mission

In an era where digital content can be easily manipulated, DeepGuard AI stands as a sentinel of authenticity. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-accuracy deepfake detection tools to empower individuals and organizations to verify the integrity of digital media.

🧠

The Model

We utilize a state-of-the-art hybrid architecture combining ResNeXt and LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) networks.

  • ResNeXt-50 extracts high-level spatial features from individual video frames.
  • LSTM analyzes the temporal sequence of these features to detect inconsistencies across time.
⚙️

The Process

Our detection pipeline operates in three precise stages:

  1. Face Extraction: Automatic detection and cropping of faces from video frames.
  2. Feature Analysis: Analyzing facial artifacts and temporal jitter.
  3. Classification: Assigning a confidence score to determine Real vs. Fake.

Built with ♥ using Django and PyTorch.
